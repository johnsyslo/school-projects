Loyola University

Why I Like It

I like Loyola for a few reasons. It is close to me at only 8.5 miles away and they have some decent sports teams. I have been there a bunch and know where a few things are already.

Tuition

The average tuiton price of Loyola University is $47,808. In 4 years, that brings you just shy of $200,000.

Requirements

Loyola has an 77% acceptence rate. To get in you need mostly all As and a 3.68 GPA or better. The average students has a SAT of between 1130-1320 or an ACT score of 27-32
